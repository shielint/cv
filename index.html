How to Make Scramble eggs

Ingrediens

  • 5 eggs
  • 2 Table spoons oil
  • 1 bunch of green onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. cut green onion into pieces
  2. scrmble the eggs and put in salt and pepper
  3. pour oil into frying pan and heat
  4. put cut green onion into pan
  5. put eggs into pan
